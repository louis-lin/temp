Space Telescopes
================

Space telescopes are instruments that orbit above Earth's atmosphere, providing clearer views of the universe.

Hubble Space Telescope
----------------------
Launched in 1990, Hubble has been instrumental in numerous astronomical discoveries.

James Webb Space Telescope
--------------------------
Launched in 2021, the James Webb Space Telescope is the largest and most powerful space telescope ever built.

Kepler Space Telescope
----------------------
Operational from 2009 to 2018, Kepler was designed to survey a portion of the Milky Way galaxy in search of exoplanets.
